The Splunk Add-on for Sophos allows a Splunk software administrator to collect Sophos Endpoint Security events and map them to the Splunk CIM. You can then use the data with other Splunk apps, such as Splunk Enterprise Security and the Splunk App for PCI Compliance. 

Download the Splunk Add-on for Sophos from Splunkbase.
