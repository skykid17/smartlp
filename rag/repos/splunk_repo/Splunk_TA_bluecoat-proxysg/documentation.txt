The Splunk Add-on for Symantec Blue Coat ProxySG lets a Splunk software administrator collect data from Blue Coat ProxySG log files in W3C ELFF format. This add-on provides the inputs and CIM-compatible knowledge to use with other Splunk apps, such as Splunk Enterprise Security and the Splunk App for PCI Compliance. 

Download the Splunk Add-on for Symantec Blue Coat ProxySG from Splunkbase.
